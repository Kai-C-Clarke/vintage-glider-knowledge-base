## What We're Looking For

Everything is welcome. Nothing is too obscure. In particular:

- **Original manufacturer documentation** — flight manuals, maintenance manuals, rigging guides, parts lists
- **Type-specific technical notes** — anything not in the official manual, passed down through clubs or instructors
- **Maintenance and repair records** — inspection logs, repair schemes, BGA 205 records
- **Photographs** — construction details, repairs, fabric work, fittings, hardware
- **Build records** — original or restoration
- **Correspondence** — letters from manufacturers, BGA technical notes, historic airworthiness paperwork
- **Anything handwritten** — workshop notes, measurements, material specifications

### Types of particular interest
Olympia 2b · Grunau Baby · Prefect · Tutor · Skylark · Swallow · Ka-6 · K-8 · K-13 · SHK-1 · Minimoa · Rhönbussard · Bergfalke · Weihe · Condor · Kranich · Meise · Ceilidh · Dart · Kite · Petrel · Capstan · Bocian

...but honestly, if it flies on a wire and was built before 1980, we want to know about it.

## How This Will Be Used

This archive is being built to support **CURIOUS** — an AI-assisted workshop system for vintage glider maintenance and airworthiness work developed by Jon Stiles (BGA Inspector I/C1408, Robertsbridge, East Sussex).

The goal is to make scattered technical knowledge searchable, cross-referenceable, and accessible to inspectors and restorers who might otherwise spend years tracking it down — or never find it at all.

All contributions will be credited. Nothing will be used commercially.
