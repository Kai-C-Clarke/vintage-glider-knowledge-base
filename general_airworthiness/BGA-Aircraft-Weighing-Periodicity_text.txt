COVID19 AND BGA AIRCRAFT WEIGHING PERIODICITY REQUIREMENT

The COVID19 restrictions are severely limiting access to maintenance facilities, including
aircraft weighing facilities.

The BGA Airworthiness Compendium ‘General information all types. Item 20. Weight and
Balance’ states that ‘all types are normally to be re-weighed every eight years.’

With immediate effect, that specific requirement is modified to ‘all types are normally to be
re-weighed every nine years.’
